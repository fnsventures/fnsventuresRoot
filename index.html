About Us

F & S Ventures is a partnership firm promoted by Subhramonyu Das and Flora Priyadarsini. We build trusted, community-focused businesses with a long-term vision.

Meet the Founders

Subhramonyu Das & Flora Priyadarsini

Husband and wife, software engineers, and partners in business—we founded F & S Ventures to combine technical discipline with hands-on entrepreneurship in our community.

From our first venture in energy retail to planned growth in wellness and sustainable farming, we focus on quality, transparency, and lasting value for the people we serve.
